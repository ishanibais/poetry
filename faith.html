MY POETRY CORNER

FAITH

Faith...
How shall I define faith?
When you look up in the sky and pray to something,
That something is faith.
When you learn to walk and sense your parents around,
That sense of security in you is faith.
When a person in front of a black board with a white chalk tell you
that A stands for apple,
The trust in you is faith.
When those old wrinkled eyes see you coming
and hope you will sit and talk with them,
That hope is faith.
Faith is something you have in yourself,
Something that gives you strength,
Something that keeps you going.
If you don't have that faith in you,
You would end up with a bucket full of regrets,
a few good memories
And no life in it!

-Ishani
