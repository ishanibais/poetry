MY POETRY CORNER

OUR COLLEGES

Our colleges have started just without the colleges,
This wait to get there, already feels like ages.
A photo with that white building is what we need,
A freshers party is on the list indeed.
We want to meet, just without the GOOGLE in it,
Not in teams, but in the classrooms we wanna sit.
Hostel life...that not so famous mess food...
college fests...late night assignments...
some amazing memories...and what not...
You name it and we are already missing that dot...
This beautiful campus feels so near...
Yet so far!

-Ishani
