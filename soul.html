MY POETRY CORNER

A SOUL

A soul on the ceiling or blood on floors,
each has an untold story to tell.
Story of a fighter who fought,
fought till he realized he was the only one who could be defeated,
fought till he realized he was the only one who could be killed!
The shield of love was not enough for the sword of circumstances.
The sword killed and the shield lied broken,
lied broken in the same blood, which it was protecting for years!
People didn't know what sharpened the sword
"You did!", smiled the soul..!

-Ishani
