MY POETRY CORNER

NO REGRETS

On that plate was a beautiful triangle
With veggies all around and cheese oozing out,
as if shouting and compelling me to eat it.
Every second spent in gym,
Every sacrifice my tastebuds have made,
Stopped me, my heart and my hands.
But that damm triangle was at its best,
It was wanting love from me,
And just a little gaze I could give!
Oh yes I have strong heart...I didn't eat
Because I would never want to regret eating that
little triangle with veggies all around
and cheese oozing out!

-Ishani
