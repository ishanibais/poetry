MY POETRY CORNER

2020

Hey 2020!
You have come to an end now
Your platter had many flavors,
Everything from sweet to sour, from bitter to spicy
You united some but separated others
You had lessons for some and punishment for others
We wished you to be a HAPPY new year,
But your take on happiness was not same as ours!
From the pandemic to wildfires,
From floods to droughts,
From quarantine time to family hours,
From masked smiled to depression,
From elections to economic recession.
Oh dear! You were difficult af!
And so,
Roman gods were spotted together in the sky..
To bid you a good Bye!

-Ishani
